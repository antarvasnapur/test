DMCA Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Copyright Policy

Antarvasnapur respects intellectual property rights and responds to valid DMCA notices within 5–10 business days.

How to File a Notice

Email dmca@antarvasnapur.in with the following:

  • Your contact information (name, address, email)
  • Description of the copyrighted work you claim is infringed
  • URL of the allegedly infringing content
  • Statement of good-faith belief that the use is unauthorised
  • Statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, email us with your details and a statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was a mistake.

Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to terminate access for repeat infringers.
